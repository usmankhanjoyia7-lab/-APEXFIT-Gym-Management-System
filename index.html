Got Questions

FREQUENTLY
ASKED

What are your opening hours? +
APEXFIT operates two daily sessions: Morning from 6:00 AM to 10:00 AM, and Evening from 7:00 PM to 11:00 PM — seven days a week.
What are the membership prices? +
We offer three plans: Basic (PKR 3,000/mo) with gym access and locker, Pro (PKR 5,000/mo) with trainer support and diet guide, and Elite (PKR 8,000/mo) with a personal trainer, custom workout plan, advanced diet, and progress tracking.
Where is APEXFIT located? +
APEXFIT is located in the Commercial Area, Bahawalpur, Punjab, Pakistan. You can find us on Google Maps or call +92 370 3523316 for directions.
How do I join or book a session? +
The easiest way is to WhatsApp us at +92 370 3523316 or fill in the contact form below. You can also call us directly during staffed hours.
Do you provide diet plans? +
Yes! Pro members receive a personalized diet guide. Elite members receive a full advanced diet plan tailored to their specific body goals and food preferences.
Is APEXFIT suitable for beginners? +
Absolutely. Our trainers specialize in guiding beginners from their very first workout. The Basic and Pro plans are ideal for anyone just starting their fitness journey.
